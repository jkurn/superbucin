## Problem Statement

Jonathan wants to build a Hago-style minigame collection called SUPERBUCIN for him and his girlfriend Pricylia. The platform is a mobile-web game lobby that hosts casual multiplayer minigames. Game #1 is **Pig vs Chick** — a real-time auto-battler inspired by Hago's Sheep Fight, where two players spawn units that march and clash.

## What Makes This Cool

This isn't a generic Hago clone — it's a **personalized couples game** dripping with inside jokes. The pig vs chicken theming, the pet names baked into UI copy, the personality of the lobby — it's a love letter disguised as a game. Pricylia opens a link, picks her side, and suddenly she's commanding an army of 🐤🐔🐓 against Jonathan's 🐷🐗 horde. The "whoa" moment is realizing this whole thing was built just for them.

The Mario Party-style collection shell means every new minigame is a surprise drop — a gift that keeps giving.

## Constraints

- **Mobile-web only** — must work great in mobile browsers (iOS Safari, Android Chrome). No app store.
- **Real-time multiplayer** — both players online simultaneously, low-latency unit spawning and combat.
- **Built for two** — no matchmaking or auth to start. Room code or shared link to connect.
- **Hago mechanics faithfully adapted** — auto-march, tap-to-spawn, energy system, unit collision, last team standing.
- **Three.js rendering** — use Three.js as the game engine for rendering, effects, and scene management.
- **Expandable** — architecture must support adding new minigames to the collection without major refactoring.

## Premises

1. SUPERBUCIN is a mobile-web app hosted on the web, played in mobile browsers. No app store.
2. Real-time multiplayer is required — both players online, spawning units live.
3. The shell is a game lobby/collection (like Hago's home screen). Pig vs Chick lives inside it. Future games slot in.
4. Pig vs Chick follows Hago Sheep Fight mechanics — auto-marching units, tap to spawn, units collide, energy system, last team standing wins.
5. Symmetric gameplay, asymmetric skins — pig side and chicken side have identical stats per tier (4 tiers). Players choose their side before each match.
6. Built for two first, shareable with friends later.
7. The personality layer (pet names, inside jokes, UI copy) is what makes it SUPERBUCIN.

## Approaches Considered

### Approach A: Vanilla Canvas + Socket.io
Fastest prototype, but no structure for game collection growth. Manual UI work for lobby.

### Approach B: Phaser.js + Socket.io
Purpose-built 2D game framework. Clean scene system for multiple games. But adds learning curve and heavier bundle — overkill for tap-to-spawn mechanics.

### Approach C: Three.js + Socket.io (SELECTED)
Three.js handles rendering with room for visual flair (particles, 3D lobby, camera transitions). Socket.io for real-time multiplayer. Node.js backend for game rooms and state. Scales to 3D elements in future minigames. Full control over the game loop.

## Recommended Approach

**Three.js + Socket.io + Node.js backend.**

### Pig vs Chick — Game Design

**Unit Tiers (symmetric stats, different skins):**

| Tier | Chicken Side | Pig Side | HP | ATK | Speed | Energy Cost |
|------|-------------|----------|-----|-----|-------|-------------|
| 1 | 🐤 Chick | 🐷 Piglet | 30 | 10 | Fast | 1 |
| 2 | 🐥 Young Hen | 🐖 Young Pig | 60 | 20 | Medium | 2 |
| 3 | 🐔 Chicken | 🐗 Boar | 100 | 35 | Medium | 3 |
| 4 | 🐓 Rooster | 🦏 Big Boar | 180 | 50 | Slow | 5 |

**Core Mechanics:**
- **Energy system:** Both players start with 0 energy. Energy regenerates at ~1/second. Max energy: 10.
- **Spawning:** Tap a unit button to spend energy and spawn that tier. Unit appears at your base.
- **Auto-march:** Units walk toward the opponent's side automatically.
- **Collision:** When opposing units meet, they fight. Damage is dealt simultaneously each tick. The surviving unit continues marching with remaining HP.
- **Win condition:** First player to get a unit past the opponent's base wins the round. Best of 3 rounds wins the match.
- **Lane system:** Single lane (like Sheep Fight). Units queue and fight head-to-head.

**Match Flow:**
1. Both players join room via code/link
2. Side selection screen — pick Pig or Chicken
3. 3-second countdown
4. Game starts — energy fills, spawn units, watch them clash
5. Round ends when a unit crosses the opponent's base line
6. Best of 3 → victory screen with celebration animation

**Personality Layer:**
- Victory screen: "SAYANG MENANG! 🤣🤣🤣" / "GG LUCU~"
- Lobby greeting: Uses pet names (sayang, lucu)
- Sound effects: Cute oink/cluck sounds on spawn
- Room code format: Fun words instead of random strings (e.g., "JONACHU-1234")
- 11:11 easter egg: If you play at 11:11, special animation
